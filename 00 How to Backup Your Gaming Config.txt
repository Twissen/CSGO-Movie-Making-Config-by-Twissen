Get into the game. Open the console and type host_writeconfig
Close the game.
Go to this steam directory.
C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\userdata\[YOURSTEAMID]
and copy 730 folder to somewhere safe.

Also, go to Counter Strike Global Offensive local files directory.
SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo
and copy "cfg" folder to somewhere safe.
That's it!

!Please be aware that this backup process may not backup all of your settings!
You should have own gaming config to be able to sure that everything is backed up.
